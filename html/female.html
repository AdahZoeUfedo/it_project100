Our Hostels

Female Hostels

Discover a wide range of our hostels where you can rest and be in a home away from home

Pa-etos

Pa-etos was made specially for rich females. and is sold at the price of N360 only.This hostel allows for double and quad occupancy for our students. Facilities and services available in this hostel include wardrobe, bathroom and toilet facilities with functional heater, hair dryer, gas cooker and a well functional water heater.

Kelson

The Kelson hostel is nearly as good as the Pa-etos hostel. It offers basic and necessary student facilities like mattress, wardrobe and bathroom utilities. Everything in the Kelson hostel is made of the finest qualities of Mexican materials and it comes at a cheap price of N350 for you

CICL

Though CICL hostel may not be good as Pa-etos or Kelson, it offers basic needs for comfort and peace. The price is as cheap as N240

Stanzel

Though the standard of this hostel does not come close to Pa-etos, it is a clean and comfortable hostel where students can rest in peace and it comes at a cheap price of N180 for you
